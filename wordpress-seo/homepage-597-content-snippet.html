What is Inthecircle?

Inthecircle is a creator networking app. You can meet other creators. You can team up on projects. You can grow your circle in a trusted community.

What you can do here

First, you can browse profiles that fit your niche. Next, you can use the Board for collabs and open calls. Also, you can keep chats in one place. In addition, you can join moments that feel small and real. For example, you might find an editor, a host, or a brand partner. However, the goal is not vanity metrics. Therefore, we focus on ties that help you over time.

Who it is for

Likewise, many kinds of creators can join. You might be a streamer or a podcaster. You might be a designer or a photographer. Moreover, you can be early in your path. Meanwhile, you can still share what you work on. Thus, you get clearer fit with people who care about the same craft.
